About us

More than a stay,
a true Sicilian experience

At Magdadom, we believe that where you stay shapes how you experience a place. That’s why we carefully select each home, not just for its beauty, but for its character, location, and the feeling it creates the moment you walk in.

Our properties are set in some of Sicily’s most captivating destinations — from the vibrant coastal life of Giardini Naxos to the timeless elegance of Taormina and the historic charm of Siracusa. Each home is personally managed and thoughtfully maintained, ensuring a seamless and comfortable stay.

W Whether you are looking for a quiet retreat by the sea, a cultural escape, or simply a place to slow down and enjoy the Sicilian rhythm of life, Magdadom offers more than accommodation — it offers a sense of belonging, even if just for a few days.
